The difference is visible

Stop guessing which version to keep.

MediaLens groups exact duplicates and close variants, then explains what changed so cleanup decisions are reviewable instead of risky.

  • Exact and perceptual duplicate detection
  • Configurable keep/delete priorities
  • Side-by-side comparison with reveal slider
  • Safe review before delete or retention cleanup

02
